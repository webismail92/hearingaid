Why can't I hear?

Early signs of hearing loss can be subtle. Can you hear people but not always understand what they are saying? This is a common symptom of hearing loss. Why? In 90% of all cases, hearing loss occurs when the delicate hair cells in the inner ear are damaged or not functioning properly.
